## 4. Practical Information

### Money
| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Currency | Canadian Dollar (CAD) · $1 CAD ≈ $0.73 USD |
| ATMs | Widely available · your US debit card works at most ATMs |
| Credit cards | Accepted everywhere · Visa/Mastercard preferred |
| Tipping | 18–20% at restaurants standard in Canada |

### Baby Gear Notes
- **Carrier** — essential for Rockpile at Moraine Lake, Takakkaw Falls, Rogers Pass walks
- **Stroller** — works on Bow River Trail, Bow Falls, Lake Louise Lakeshore, Emerald Lake, Cascade Ponds
- **Layers for Addie** — lakes are 5–10°C cooler than Banff town (35–50°F mornings)
- **Sunscreen** — high elevation = stronger UV even on cloudy days

### Emergency
| Service | Number |
|---------|--------|
| Emergency (Canada) | 911 |
| Banff Mineral Springs Hospital | +1 (403) 762-2222 |
| Parks Canada Dispatch | +1 (403) 762-4506 |

### Cell Coverage
- Rogers/Bell/Telus have coverage in Banff and Golden town areas
- **Coverage drops** on Icefields Parkway and inside Yoho NP — download offline maps on Google Maps or Maps.me before leaving each morning
