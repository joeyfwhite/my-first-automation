3 Practical Information

Money

  • Currency: CAD ($1 ≈ $0.73 USD)
  • Credit cards accepted everywhere
  • Tipping: 18–20% standard
  • ATMs widely available in town

Baby Gear — What to Bring Daily

  • Carrier — essential for Moraine Lake, Takakkaw Falls, Rogers Pass
  • Stroller — Bow River, Lake Louise, Emerald Lake, Cascade Ponds
  • Warm layers for Addie (lakes are 35–50°F mornings)
  • Sunscreen (high elevation = stronger UV)
  • Carrier rain cover

Cell Coverage

  • Good in Banff and Golden towns
  • Drops on Icefields Parkway + Yoho NP
  • Download offline maps before each morning
  • Google Maps or Maps.me recommended

Weather (June)

  • Banff town: 55–70°F daytime
  • Lakes + trailheads: 35–50°F mornings
  • Layer everything — changes fast
  • Rain jacket always in the bag
